## Deutsch Learning Rules Infinitiv Verb

A-IE-A 

| Infinitiv | Pråteritum |    Perfekt | English Meaning |
|:----------|:-----------|-----------:|-----------------|
| fallen    | Fiel       |   gefallen | to fall         |
| lassen    | ließ       |   gelassen | to let          |
| halten    | hielt      |   gehalten | to hold         |
| schlafen  | schlief    | geschlafen | to sleep        |


AU-IE-AU

| Infinitiv | Pråteritum |  Perfekt | English Meaning |
|:----------|:-----------|---------:|-----------------|
| laufen    | lief       | gelaufen | to run          |


EI-IE-EI

| Infinitiv | Pråteritum |  Perfekt | English Meaning |
|:----------|:-----------|---------:|-----------------|
| heißen    | hieß       | geheißen | to be called    |


U-IE-U

| Infinitiv | Pråteritum | Perfekt | English Meaning           |
|:----------|:-----------|--------:|---------------------------|
| rufen     | rief       | gerufen | to shout out/ to call out |

A-I-A

| Infinitiv | Pråteritum |  Perfekt | English Meaning |
|:----------|:-----------|---------:|-----------------|
| fangen    | fing       | gefangen | to catch        |
| hången    | hing       | gehangen | to hang         |



#### What is the difference between regular verbs and irregular verbs?



> Here is the idea `tip`. {: .prompt-tip }

The regular verbs have a predictable pattern and irregular doesn't have any specific pattern. Though some group of irregular verbs can have similar pattern just like above mentioned
example.
